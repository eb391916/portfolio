Design

I only recently discovered my love for design. I entered college as a biology major, but soon decided that it wasn't the career path for me.

After meeting with an advisor, I discovered Ohio University's School of Visual Communication and ended up transferring into their interactive multimedia program.

From there I discovered that my true passion was design. I love working with websites and user experience, posters are so much fun to create, and typography is a topic I love to discuss.

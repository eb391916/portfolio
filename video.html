Video

Most of my video and audio experience comes from taking a multimedia production course. I discovered that although it isn't my passion, I don't mind creating video pieces.

Disruption

Disruption is a piece I created as a final project in my multimedia production class. It's a story about a retired special education teacher whose property is going to be temporarily destroyed by a natural gas pipeline.

A Gift for Life

A Gift for Life is another project from my multimedia production class. It feature Dr. Jeremy Burke, a classical saxophonist and the founder of Playing for Life. Dr. Burke discusses how the program started, and how he uses music as a way to talk about organ donation.
